Our Leadership

Bishop Dr. James K. Kimemia

Under the visionary leadership of Bishop Dr. James K. Kimemia, JCC Naivasha has become a beacon of light in the heart of Kenya. Our mission is to transform lives through the gospel of Jesus Christ, emphasizing the power of the Holy Spirit, the love of God, and the hope found only in Him.

We believe in a holistic approach to ministry, nurturing the spiritual, emotional, and social needs of our congregation and the wider Naivasha community.
